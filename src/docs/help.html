Usage Instruction:

Notes

Product Data: Some typical product data are provided, but they may be based on estimated values. Users may create custom inputs for any specific values required.

Predefined Building Parts: Parts are based on NZS 3604 and will suit most residential and light-commercial cases.

Building Dimensions: The definitions of Dimension-NS and Dimension-EW refer to the total length of the building in the North–South or East–West directions respectively.

These are illustrated in the figure below:

Note: Wind from the NS direction acts on the EW walls, and wind from the EW direction acts on the NS walls.

Lateral Loads: Earthquake actions follow NZS 1170.5. Wind actions are based on NZS 3604, with the option of a simplified method from NZS 1170.2. Users may design bracing for either method or the maximum wind actions obtained.

Bracing Layout & Design: Bracing must comply with the provisions of NZS 3604.
